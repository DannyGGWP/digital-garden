# Warm Ups🔥
---
Posture is IMPORTANT. Keeping a neutral wrist position is important. Circles are always a good warm up. They also can be gesture sketches, thumbnails of what you are doing etc. 

> [!Tip] Keep it short and sweet
> don't spend too much time on this. 5 minutes is plenty 

# Anatomy 🩻
---
Proportion the fraction of the body that a part takes up. 
The head can essentially be broken down into two equally sized circles. 
body is roughly 8 heads tall. 
The torso is roughly half the length of the body. 
The elbows are at the same level at the waist. 
The waist is about 3 heads down the body. 
Pelvis is about one head high 
Hands are about one head long. (That's HUGE!)
they end about halfway down the thigh. 
thighs are about 2 heads long. 
The hip bone pushes the top of the thigh outwards from the pelvis.

# Head proportions 🤯
---
eyes are about 1/4 of the way between 
## Eyes 
The tear duct is right below the halfway point of the eye. 
the lens of the eye causes the upper lid to deform around it and slightly overlaps the top of the iris.
