## The Grind ☕
To learn to draw the head one must draw from every angle. This applies to basically everything but I digress. 

A good place to start is the human skull 💀. [Here](https://sketchfab.com/3d-models/shapes-of-the-human-skull-4868b0bea3b94041b77ecadf6f9cdf0e) is a good 3D model of one to practice on. 
Once you've learned the bones you are ready to graduate to the planes of the face. For this the [[./Asaro Head|Asaro Head]] is useful. 

## The eyes of Dr. TJ Eckleburg 👁️
Lets talk eyes. Mainly this breaks down to one big tip 
> [!tip] keep perspective in mind!!
> Make sure to draw the eyes in perspective. this helps convey a sense of realism even in stylized drawings

# Master studies. 🙇
Study the Masters, (Ok maybe just people you like!). Try to understand what they are doing in their work and what you like about it. 
>[!tip] this also works for photo reference!
>Identify what you like about a reference and then try and highlight that in your art!
